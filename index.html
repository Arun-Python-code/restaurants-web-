Our Culinary Saga

THE STORY

At Siruvaani Restaurant, we bring you the soul of Kongu Nadu with an authentic virundhu experience — served traditionally on vaazhai ilai and in man paanai, yet within the comforts of a luxurious 5-star ambiance. It’s where rich tradition meets refined elegance, creating a dining moment you’ll never forget.

Located within Holiday Residency, Coimbatore’s premier hotel, Siruvaani offers more than just a meal — it’s a celebration of heritage, flavour, and hospitality.

THE TASTE OF SIRUVAANI

Holiday Residency also houses Zen Park, a sophisticated urban retreat that complements your visit with serene luxury and modern charm. Come, savour the taste of Kongu Nadu — only at Siruvaani.
